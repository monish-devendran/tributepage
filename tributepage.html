Steve Jobs

( 1955 - 2011 )

"Creativity is just connecting things"

Biography:


Steven Paul Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, to Joanne Schieble (later Joanne Simpson) and Abdulfattah "John" Jandali, two University of Wisconsin graduate students who gave their unnamed son up for adoption. As a boy, Jobs and his father worked on electronics in the family garage.
